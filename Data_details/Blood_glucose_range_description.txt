For pre-prandial (before meals) blood sugar, the target range is 4.0 to 7.0 mmol/L. A reading within this range is considered in range. A reading below 4.0 mmol/L is below range, while a reading above 7.0 mmol/L is out of range.

For 2-hour post-prandial (after eating) blood sugar, the target range is 5.0 to 10.0 mmol/L. A reading within this range is considered in range. A reading below 5.0 mmol/L is below range, while a reading above 10.0 mmol/L is out of range.
